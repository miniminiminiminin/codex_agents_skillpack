# CEO To CTO Intake

## Decision

- gate: is the directive ready for `CTO` planning
- decision owner: `CTO`

## Required Evidence

- business goal
- scope guardrails
- approval constraints
- current graph context
- impact scope
- upstream dependencies
- downstream dependencies

## Loop

1. Restate the directive as a technical objective and success condition.
2. Consult current graph artifacts before decomposition.
3. Record impact scope, dependency direction, decision rights, and required approvers.
4. Decide `ready for planning`, `hold for missing context`, or `redirect to shared asset update`.

## Close When

- `CEO` intent is concrete enough to plan
- `CTO` owns the next artifact
- approval path is explicit
- graph obligations are explicit

## Hold When

- impact scope is unclear
- dependency direction is unclear
- approval owner is missing
- a shared rule is required but not yet codified
